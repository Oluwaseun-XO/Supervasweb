FAQ’S

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does SuperVas Technology provide?

We design and develop custom web applications, mobile apps (Android & iOS), and backend systems. Our team also offers UI/UX design, engineering, product management, project management and ongoing maintenance to keep your digital products running smoothly.

Do you work with startups as well as established companies?

Yes. We partner with organizations of all sizes, from early stage startups that need an MVP to established enterprises seeking to modernize or scale their digital solutions.

How long does it take to build a mobile or web app?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and features. A simple app might take 3-6 weeks, while a larger, multi feature platform can require several months. We provide a detailed project plan and milestones after our initial consultation.

What technologies do you use?

Our engineering team works with a wide range of modern technologies, including Flutter, React, Java/Kotlin, Node.js, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure. We choose the best stack to match your product goals, scalability needs, and budget.

Do you provide post launch support and maintenance?

Absolutely. We offer flexible support packages for updates, performance monitoring, security patches, and feature enhancements to ensure your product remains stable and competitive.

How do you handle project management and communication?

Our product management team uses agile methodologies and tools like ClickUp, Jira, or Trello. You’ll have a dedicated project manager, regular progress updates, and clear communication channels to keep everything on track.
